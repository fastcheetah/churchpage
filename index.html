About Our Church

Grace Community Church has been serving our community for over 25 years. We are a vibrant, welcoming congregation dedicated to sharing God's love and message of hope.

Our mission is to love God, love people, and make disciples who transform communities through the power of the Gospel.

We believe in the authority of Scripture, the power of prayer, and the importance of community. Everyone is welcome here, no matter where you are on your spiritual journey.
